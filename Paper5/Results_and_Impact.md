# Results and Impact

This section presents quantitative results, practical impact, and business value from the industrial deployment of the framework.

## Quantitative Results

### Time Savings

Table 1 summarizes time savings across different analysis tasks:

**Table 1: Time Savings by Analysis Task**

| Analysis Task | Manual Time | Framework Time | Time Savings | Improvement |
|---------------|-------------|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| **Data Integration** | 2-4 hours | 15-30 minutes | 85-90% | 4-8× faster |
| **Sensitivity Analysis** | 2-3 days | 2-4 hours | 85-90% | 12-18× faster |
| **Virtual Experiments** | 1-2 weeks | 1-2 hours | 95-98% | 40-80× faster |
| **Anomaly Detection** | 4-8 hours | 30-60 minutes | 85-90% | 8-16× faster |
| **Root Cause Analysis** | 2-3 months | 1-2 weeks | 85-90% | 8-12× faster |
| **Quality Assessment** | 1-2 hours | 10-20 minutes | 70-85% | 3-6× faster |

**Key Findings:**
- **Average Time Savings**: 85-90% across all analysis tasks
- **Largest Savings**: Virtual experiments (95-98% time savings)
- **Most Frequent Use**: Quality assessment (daily use, 70-85% time savings)

### Process Development Time Reduction

**Process Optimization:**
- **Before Framework**: 6-8 weeks for new material/geometry
- **After Framework**: 4-5 weeks for new material/geometry
- **Improvement**: 35% reduction in process development time

**Root Cause Analysis:**
- **Before Framework**: 2-3 months to identify root cause
- **After Framework**: 1-2 weeks to identify root cause
- **Improvement**: 85-90% reduction in root cause analysis time

### Quality Improvements

**Defect Rate Reduction:**
- **Baseline Defect Rate**: 12% (before framework)
- **After Framework**: 8.6% (with real-time anomaly detection)
- **Improvement**: 28% reduction in defect rates

**Early Detection:**
- **Defects Detected Before Build Completion**: 65%
- **False Positive Rate**: 22% (acceptable for early detection)
- **Spatial Accuracy**: 78% of anomalies within 1 voxel (0.5 mm) of CT defects

**Scrap Rate Reduction:**
- **Baseline Scrap Rate**: 12%
- **After Framework**: 8.6%
- **Cost Savings**: ~$50,000 per month in reduced scrap and rework

### Data Preparation Efficiency

**Manual Data Preparation:**
- **Time per Build**: 2-4 hours
- **Error Rate**: 15-20% (manual errors)
- **Consistency**: Low (varies by analyst)

**Framework Data Integration:**
- **Time per Build**: 15-30 minutes
- **Error Rate**: <2% (automated validation)
- **Consistency**: High (standardized process)

**Improvement**: 85-90% reduction in data preparation time, 90% reduction in errors
